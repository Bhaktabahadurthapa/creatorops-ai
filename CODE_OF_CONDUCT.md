# CreatorOps AI Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in CreatorOps AI respectful,
welcoming, and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of background,
identity, experience, or level of technical expertise.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Give actionable feedback and assume good intent.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Respect privacy, consent, intellectual property, and voice ownership.
- Focus discussion on improving the project and supporting its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Sharing API keys, private voice recordings, uploaded media, or other secrets.
- Using project features to impersonate, deceive, or clone a voice without
  authorization.
- Disruptive conduct that prevents healthy collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, issues, or
other participation that violates these standards. Repeated or serious
violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub
profile. Do not include sensitive personal information in a public issue.
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as reasonably
possible.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in repository spaces and whenever someone is
representing CreatorOps AI in public or community discussions.
